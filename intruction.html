Quick Appointment Booking Guide

Enjoy top-tier healthcare and book appointments quickly and easily with TCP.

SELECT MEDICAL APPOINTMENT INFORMATION

  • Create or log in to your account on the website or mobile app.
  • Select "Book at hospital" or "Book with a doctor."
  • Choose the department/specialty, doctor, date, and whether using health insurance (HI) or not.
  • Select appointment details: department, doctor, date, and whether using one.
  • Enter patient information: Create a new profile or select an existing one.

CREATE / SELECT PATIENT PROFILE

  • Option 1: Scan your health insurance card.
  • Option 2: If you've visited the hospital before, enter patient code or profile number.
  • Option 3: If it's your first time, register a new profile. Provide the following: Full name, Date of Birth, Gender, Health Insurance ID/Passport, Ethnicity, Occupation, Phone number, Email, Address.

PAYMENT FOR APPOINTMENT

  • Select a payment method: QR code scan, 24/7 bank transfer, international cards, domestic ATM cards, or e-wallets.
  • Check payment details (consultation fees, testing fees, etc.) and confirm payment.
  • You can pay directly through the app or use the hospital's online banking/payment portal.

RECEIVE E-TICKET FOR APPOINTMENT

  • After successful payment, your e-ticket will be available in the app (and sent via email).
  • If booking was done via call center (1900 2115), the ticket will be sent via SMS.

EXAMINATION AND LABORATORY PROCEDURES

  • On the appointment date, go directly to the hospital reception as instructed in the e-ticket.
  • You will be guided to undergo tests based on your medical order.
  • If additional tests are needed as advised by the doctor, please return to the front desk for the relevant department (e.g., blood tests, imaging).
  • After completing the tests, return to the initial consultation area for the doctor to interpret results and provide prescriptions or next steps (e.g., medication, appointment slips).

BOOK AN APPOINTMENT

Here you can book your appointment directly by selecting your preferred doctor, date, and time.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • How do I cancel an appointment? You can cancel through the app or by calling 1900 2115.
  • Can I change my doctor? Yes, you can modify your appointment details in the app before the scheduled time.
  • Is my payment secure? Yes, we use secure payment gateways to ensure your transactions are safe.

MEDICAL CHECK-UP PROCESS

  • Step 1: Arrive at the hospital and check in at the reception.
  • Step 2: Undergo initial health screening (blood pressure, weight, etc.).
  • Step 3: Meet with the doctor for consultation.
  • Step 4: Complete any required tests and return for results.
